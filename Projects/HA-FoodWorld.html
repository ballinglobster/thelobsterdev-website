"HA: Food World" is a conceptual mobile game to help new and upcoming parents find quick and easy recipes for their families.

Developed using Unity and C# in a team of five, I contributed to the game's design, coding, and asset creation. I coded roughly half of the game's features:

  • Recipe unlocking
  • Customer interactions and feedback
  • The tutorial system
  • Level progression

3D assets

I created several 3D assets for the game using Blender, including cookware and environment props.
